chocolate |ˈCHäk(ə)lət|
noun
a food preparation in the form of a paste or solid block made from roasted and ground cacao seeds, typically sweetened: a bar of chocolate | [as modifier] : a chocolate cookie.
• a candy made of or covered with this: a box of chocolates.
• hot chocolate: I drink chocolate because it is so soothing.
• a deep brown color: [as modifier] : huge spiders, yellow and chocolate brown.
